## Weekly countdown

- **T-72 hrs:** Confirm staff rota, guest hosts, and any partner prizes.
- **T-48 hrs:** Schedule Facebook + Instagram posts, update Google Business Profile, drop teaser in WhatsApp broadcast.
- **T-24 hrs:** Print sheets, prep QR posters, test mic/speakers, stock prizes.
- **T-4 hrs:** Set room layout, check playlists, brief door team on bundle upsells.
- **Post-event (within 12 hrs):** Post winners, tag partners, add photos to shared drive, log headcount/spend.

## Content prompts per channel

- **Stories/Reels:** 15-second setup clip, “how it started vs. how it’s going” montage, winner reveal.
- **Feed posts:** Graphic on Monday, photo carousel on Thursday, testimonial tile on Sunday.
- **Email/WhatsApp:** 60-word reminder with booking link and bundle highlight.
- **In-venue:** Table talkers, chalkboard CTA, QR on till screens.

## Team comms

- Add run-sheet links in a shared WhatsApp or Slack group.
- Nominate a content captain each week to collect assets and update the planner.
- Keep a running list of prompts that landed well — reuse, don’t rewrite.
