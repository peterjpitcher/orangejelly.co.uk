# 25 Low Budget Pub Marketing Ideas That Actually Work

Marketing does not need big budgets. It needs focus, local relevance, and consistency. Instead of a long list, here are five pillars with the few ideas that move the needle.

## Pillar 1: Visibility in the community

- Post weekly in local Facebook groups.
- Keep Google Business updated with photos and offers.
- Use a clear sign at the entrance with this week's offer.

## Pillar 2: Social proof

- Share guest photos and short reviews.
- Post short recap clips after events.
- Ask for reviews with a simple in-person script.

## Pillar 3: Partnerships

- Co-host a night with a local brand.
- Offer your space for clubs and meetings.
- Run a small fundraiser for a local cause.

## Pillar 4: In-pub conversion

- Table cards that explain the weekly rhythm.
- One hero bundle that is easy to order.
- A visible booking QR code.

## Pillar 5: Retention

- A simple loyalty card.
- A weekly email or WhatsApp list.
- Personal invites to key events.

## Common mistakes

- Trying too many ideas at once.
- Posting without a clear CTA.
- Ignoring follow-up after a good night.

## Quick checklist

- One visibility action active.
- One social proof post per week.
- One partnership per quarter.
- One retention loop running.

## Mini FAQ

**How much time does this take?**
Two short sessions per week if you plan ahead.

**Do I need paid ads?**
Not at the start. Organic local visibility often wins first.
