# Keyword Plan — Tier 1 Informational Pages (2026-05-21)

Built from Google Keyword Planner (Rounds 1–2, UK, May'25–Apr'26) **blended with first-party GSC data** (`gsc-queries-2026-05.md`). Where Planner under-reports a term that GSC proves has real demand, GSC wins (noted inline). Planner volumes are coarse buckets (50/500) from a no-spend account — treat as directional; competition + actual GSC rankings are the stronger signals.

**Use:** the **Primary** of each page drives the `<title>` (front-loaded) and H1; **Secondary** terms go in the meta description, H2s, and body. Local keywords are **N/A** for these national informational pages — they're reserved for the `/pub-marketing-[county]` batch (decision D3).

## Cross-page rationale
The strategy is **win the click before chasing new rankings**: 6 of these 8 pages already rank but convert at ~1%. For pages at pos 5–8 (menu, how-to-run, buying), the lever is the **meta description** (the title already matches intent). For pages at pos 12–17 (events, social, quiz, content, refurb), front-load the exact head term in the **title** to lift both position and CTR. All targets are Low–Medium competition and OJ-relevant; the high-volume `pub quiz questions` and the declining refurb terms are deliberately not chased. Local keywords are excluded by design — they belong to the county landing pages.
