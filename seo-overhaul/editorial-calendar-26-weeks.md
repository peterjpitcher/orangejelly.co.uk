## Strategy

Articles are ordered by:
1. **Seasonal timing** — content published 2-3 months before its peak
2. **Operator intent first** — articles targeting licensees who could become clients
3. **Topical clustering** — related articles grouped to build authority in a domain
4. **Volume-weighted** — higher-volume keywords earlier in the calendar
5. **Package routing** — every article routes to a specific package via CTA
