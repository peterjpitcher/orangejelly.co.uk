**Date:** 2026-03-23
**Status:** Phase 1 -- Strategy & Research

---

## Keyword Cluster Architecture

Keywords are grouped by topic cluster and intent. Each cluster maps to a page type and priority tier.

**Intent Key:**
- **I** = Informational (learning, researching)
- **N** = Navigational (looking for a specific brand/site)
- **C** = Commercial Investigation (comparing options)
- **T** = Transactional (ready to buy/act)

**Priority Tiers:**
- **T1** = Highest priority -- target within 3 months
- **T2** = Medium priority -- target within 6 months
- **T3** = Lower priority -- target within 12 months
