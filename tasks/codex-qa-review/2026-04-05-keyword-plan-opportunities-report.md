# Keyword Plan Opportunities Report

**Reviewed:** 2026-04-05
**Plan reviewed:** `/seo-overhaul/keyword-plan.md`
**Reviewer:** SEO Strategy Audit (Claude Code)

---

## Executive Summary

The keyword plan is strong on entertainment (quiz, bingo, karaoke, games) and core marketing terms. It captures approximately 146K monthly searches. However, it has significant blind spots in compliance/legal, financial management, staffing operations, technology, drinks-specific content, additional seasonal spikes, mid-funnel comparison content, and interactive tool pages. The opportunities below could add an estimated 30,000-60,000 additional monthly searches and, more importantly, capture high-intent audiences closer to a buying decision.
